POCKET EXPENSES

Those who attend to small expenses are always rich

Managing Expenses


Personal expenses

Users can enter their daily expenses and assign categories to them, set up recurring expenses and effectively visualize statistics and break up of expenses enabling users to discipline their finances

Group expenses

If a group of people need to share the cost on a particular amount, the system will ensure that anyone who paid the bill is reimbursed. The app will provide an option to enter the amount either manually or via scanning a receipt
